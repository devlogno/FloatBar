About FloatBar Apps Always On Top

FloatBar Apps is a powerful browser extension designed to boost your productivity. It injects a retractable, non-intrusive floating sidebar into the edge of your screen, giving you instant access to your favorite web applications—no matter what page you are browsing.

With our advanced "Always-on-Top" (Picture-in-Picture) technology, you can instantly pop out web apps like messengers, AI chats, and media players into floating windows that stay above everything else on your screen.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

I believe your data is yours. FloatBar Apps does NOT collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any of your personal data.

When you use the FloatBar Apps extension, you can trust that your privacy is my top priority. Here is how I handle your information:

  • No Data Collection: I do not track your browsing history, analytics, keystrokes, or usage patterns. I have no servers, which means there is nowhere for your data to be sent.
  • 100% Local Storage: Any settings you configure—such as the names, URLs, and icons of the apps you save to your sidebar, or your preferred window dimensions—are saved exclusively on your own computer using your browser's local storage engine (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device.
  • No Accounts Required: You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide an email address to use this extension.
  • Picture-in-Picture Security: When you use the "Pop Out" feature, the extension simply acts as a secure window manager to arrange your tabs. It does not monitor or read the content inside those windows.

By installing and using the extension, you agree to this absolute privacy guarantee. If this policy ever changes (which I do not anticipate, as our architecture is fully local), I will update this page accordingly.
